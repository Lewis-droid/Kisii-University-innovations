How It Works

Simple steps to resolve any marks-related issues

01

Log In to Your Account

Access the system using your student credentials to view your academic records.

02

Identify Missing Marks

The system highlights any incomplete or missing marks (marked as "I") in your records.

03

Raise a Ticket

Submit a detailed ticket for the missing marks, including any supporting documents.

04

Track Resolution

Monitor the status of your ticket and receive notifications when your marks are updated.
